# First-run onboarding lands across the desktop app

- Date: `2026-05-04`
- Channel: `nightly`
- Version: `0.1.0-nightly.434.1`
- Release: https://github.com/cypher-asi/aura-os/releases/tag/v0.1.0-nightly.434.1

Today's nightly is a focused product drop: a complete first-run onboarding experience now ships in Aura. New users see a welcome flow, a persistent checklist that tracks five guided tasks, and a Help button in the taskbar to bring it back at any time — all wired into the AppShell across Mac, Windows, and Linux desktop builds.

## 4:33 AM — Guided first-run onboarding with welcome flow and checklist

Aura now greets new users with a two-step welcome modal and a persistent five-task checklist that tracks progress automatically and can be reopened from the taskbar.

- Added a per-user onboarding store with localStorage persistence that defines five starter tasks — send your first message, create a project, create an agent, generate an image with AURA 3D, and explore plans & credits — and tracks welcome and checklist visibility state. (`bd80420`)
- Introduced a two-step welcome modal shown on first login, featuring the AURA logo, an intro message, and a preview of the onboarding checklist, with a skip option and analytics tracking. (`988eee6`)
- Shipped a floating, collapsible checklist in the bottom-right corner with a progress bar, per-task rows that deep-link into the relevant feature, and dismiss controls so users can hide it once they're rolling. (`708e75e`)
- Tasks now complete themselves: a watcher subscribes to the message, projects, agents, AURA 3D, and billing stores to detect real activity, and a new Help button between Settings and Profile in the bottom taskbar reopens or resets the checklist on demand. (`6f91ff3`, `e5d310e`)

## Highlights

- New welcome modal and 5-task onboarding checklist
- Auto-completing tasks watch real product activity
- Help button in the taskbar reopens guidance anytime
- Shipped to Mac, Windows, and Linux nightly builds
